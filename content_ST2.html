Reducing Plastic Waste and Polution

Pollution is getting to a stage where it negatively affects the oceans, harming a habitat that covers 70% of the surface of the Earth. There should be a decrease in dumping waste in the oceans, as most plastics are not biodegradable. So they would take up to 500 years to decompose.

Efforts must be made to reduce the wastage and pick up of waste. Little by little, this issue can be solved.

Climate Change

Climate Change has been an issue that has been on the rise for the last 100 years. As more time passes, more issues will arise. One such is the rising sea level from rising temperatures, causing issues like flooding, and we can see issues where the quality of water is affecting marine life to suffer as well. As a race, we need to ensure we reduce our CO2 emissions as low as possible. Also asking for policy changes to make sure that governments and organisations are aware of these issues and help to solve them.

Educating others is a powerful tool that will help this planet alot and will mean alot and has the potential to improve our quality of life but also the quality of life for marine animals and land animals.

Sustainable Practices

Sustainable practices are a way to help the marine world without compromising your day-to-day lives. There are shops like ours that will sell normal day-to-day items that are commonly thrown away. They are tested and made sure that they are made from fully recycled plastics which does not reduce or harm the quality of the product. There are also items where it is biodegradable that look just like normal products. When in contact with water for a decent amount of time will just dissolve into the water and never be seen again.

This is a win-win situation where our product that are thrown away are tested and made sure that it will cause as little harm as possible to marine life

Marine Life

Marine life is one of the most important for not only us but for the world. Having a healthy ocean for the world will make a massive impact. So respecting the ocean is another step to making it better. Going on trips should come with good practice, not harming any marine life, and cleaning after your selfs. There should be zones where some endangered marine life should be away from reach for a period as they need to be taken care of.

As these Marine Protected Areas (MPA's) are another possibility as well.
