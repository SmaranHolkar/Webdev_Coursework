Content Student 4. Cleaning ocean for sealife

How plastic affects sealife?

Sealife does get affected by plastic in the ocean. Firstly they can get tangled in plastic and may die due to othe sealife. They may die to to choking on the plastic.

Plastic waste can encourage the growth of pathogens in the ocean. According to a recent study, scientists concluded that corals that come into contact with plastic have an 89 percent chance of contracting disease, compared with a 4 percent likelihood for corals that do not.( source. )

Oceancleanup

The ocean cleanup are using huge boats and nets to collect plastic from oceans and rivers. Despite encountering some mishaps and criticism regarding its efficacy, environmental effects, and the transparency between its operations , The Ocean Cleanup organization is dedicated to combating ocean plastic pollution on a global scale. The organization continues to develop technology to tackle this critical environmental concern.

How to help?

Helping clean beaches and rivers can help ecosystems flourish again. Start of small by using whatever you can to pick up plastic from beaches.

Use biodegradable or reuable products, such as, bottles, spoons, straws. As biodegradable products can break over down quicker than regular plastic products, it will not make a negative impact on sealife.
